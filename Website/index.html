Hi! My name is Josh.

I'm a game developer currently studying Computer Science and Digital Narrative & Interactive Design at the University of Pittsburgh.

STEELPUNK is an MMO FPS (massively-multiplayer online first-person shooter) about reclaiming the Earth from a planet-conquering artificial intelligence.

As a co-founder and gameplay programmer at BLAST FURNACE GAMES, I'm leading the development of Steelpunk alongside a small team of inspired developers spun out of Carnegie Mellon University's Game Creation Society.

We utilize rollback netcode and lag compensation to maximize performance and reduce latency to deliver an exceptional networked multiplayer experience.

As an Application Engineer Intern at TerraSim, I shipped contributions to Mantle ETM, a simulated terrain creation pipeline and management platform supporting VBS4, real-time 3D combat simulation game engine.

Feel free to check out The Counsel in The Cave or any of my other games!
